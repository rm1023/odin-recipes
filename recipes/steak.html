Description

Here is a very, very complicated recipe to cook part of a cow. You probably won't do it right. Don't sweat it, not everyone is gifted.

Ingredients

  • Favorite cut of steak
  • A bit of salt
  • A dash of pepper
  • Probably some butter
  • BBQ Saus

Steps

  1. Turn on the grill
  2. Put cow on grill
  3. Flip cow after 3 minutes
  4. Drip butter on cow
  5. Enjoy
