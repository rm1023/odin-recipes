Chocolate Cake

Main Menu

Description

I've tried and failed for years to make a chocolate cake better than the one you buy at Costco. This recipe is an acknowledgement of defeat and includes instructions on how to buy this at Costco.

Ingredients

  • Car
  • Gas
  • Money
  • Hands

Steps

  1. Turn on the car
  2. Drive to Costco
  3. Find the chocolate cake (feel free to indulge in some free samples along the way)
  4. Buy the chocolate cake
  5. Go home and eat the whole thing yourself
