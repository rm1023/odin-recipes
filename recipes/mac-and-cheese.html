Mac And Cheese

Description

Here is a simple recipe to make mouth-watering macaroni and cheese for Ryan to eat! This recipe will surely impress your friends, family, and foes with its creaminess and cheesiness galore. Enjoy and please leave a review!

Ingredients

  • 32 oz. of macaroni (I use Barilla)
  • 120 oz. of cheeeeese
  • milk
  • butter
  • parsley
  • some paprika
  • a spoonful of love

Steps

  1. Boil water at 273K
  2. Dump cheese into water
  3. Crunch macaroni up into little pieces
  4. Melt butter in a pan
  5. Drink the milk
  6. Once cheese has sufficiently boiled, use it to glue the macaroni bits together
  7. Grind up parsley and paprika using a mortar and pestle
  8. Snort parsley and parika combo, enjoy dish
